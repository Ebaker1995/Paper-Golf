    Game Rules

    1. Roll the dice to determine the number of spaces you can move.

    2. Click on a highlighted position to move the ball.

    3. The goal is to get the ball into the hole with the fewest moves.

    4. If the ball is on the fairway, it will move one extra space in the direction you choose.

    5. The game ends when the ball reaches the hole.
